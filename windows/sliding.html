SLIDING WINDOWS

Make life simpler while lowering your heating & cooling costs with energy efficient sliding replacement windows from Renewal by Andersen® of Seattle.

These easy to use windows are also easy to maintain, thanks to Renewal by Andersen’s® exclusive Fibrex® frame material.

Lower quality sliding windows can post problems. Typical sliding window are often stuck in their tracks or jammed. But our Fibrex material means that your sliding windows will glide on their track smoothly, with virtually no required maintenance. When the time does come to clean them, they can be easily lifted from their track, wiped clean, and then locked back in place.

Renewal by Andersen sliding windows can help to increase ventilation in your home, opened and closed as wide as you’d like to adapt to changes in weather. They are available with Renewal by Andersen’s exclusive TruScene® insect screens, which are made with a micro-fine stainless steel mesh that’s one-third the diameter of our standard screen wire to let fresh air and sunshine in and keep the smallest insects out.

Every Renewal by Andersen window utilizes High Performance™ Low-E4® glass in order to help homeowners save money on their monthly energy bills. They are also backed by one of the strongest window and door replacement limited warranties you will find.**
